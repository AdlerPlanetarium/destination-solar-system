Moon

Moved by the Moon’s stark beauty and history

EXHIBITIONS

Share in the story of America’s first journeys into space as told through the powerful narrative of Jim Lovell in the Shoot for the Moon gallery. Learn how Lovell and others risked their lives to advance scientific understanding when human space flight was still in its infancy. Discover the Gemini 12 spacecraft and the awe-inspiring moments Lovell and Buzz Aldrin shared during their three days in space. Focus on the historic accomplishments of the hugely successful Apollo missions, which saw America reach its goal of landing a man on the Moon.

SKY SHOWS

In case you haven’t heard, the Moon is trending again...and not in a retro kind of way. Like in the glory days of the 1960s and 1970s, our nearest neighbor in the sky is enjoying the attention of lunar explorers. Continue to explore its history and discover the future of lunar exploration in Back to the Moon for Good, in the Definiti Space Theater.

CITIZEN SCIENCE

Start exploring lunar geology today with the Citizen Science project, Moon Zoo, and assist in our understanding of the Moon. Help impact lunar exploration by studying the lunar surface in unprecedented detail. What once would have taken science teams 100 years to analyze now only takes a few weeks or months.

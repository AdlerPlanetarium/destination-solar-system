DESTINATION SOLAR SYSTEM

Explore the best of the Solar System in one unforgettable 30 minute tour. Discover the distant moons of Jupiter, take in the sights of the asteroid belt, awaken your senses with a cruise through Saturn’s icy rings. Space Express Tours are a great way to explore new places or see a new side of a familiar destination.

Explore the Stops

After touring the Solar System with Space Epress, spend the rest of the day exploring the Adler on your own. Adler’s historic collection, interesting shows and wonderful exhibitions will further compliment your space travels. We have a variety of recommendations to customize your trip and make it even more special.
